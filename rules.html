Hostel Rules & Regulations

  1. ADMISSION
    1. Admission to hostel is given after completing prescribed procedure.
    2. Selection for admission to the hostel shall be made by the Management of the Hostel. Every candidate will be called for an interview along with his parents/local guardian and all the required documents. After scrutiny he will be informed about the decision.
    3. Documents Required at the time of Admission:
      • Aadhar Card: (Clear photocopy) of the student and of the Parents and Local Guardians.
      • Hostel Admission Form duly filled and signed by the Parents/Guardians.
      • Medical Fitness Certificate from a registered Medical practitioner.
    4. Admission to the hostel and allocation of rooms is done by the hostel warden, as per the instructions of the management
    5. Caution Deposit and Refund:
      • Each student is required to pay a caution Deposit of Rs. 10,000/- at the time of admission into the hostel.
      • At the time of leaving the hostel, the student must make a written application for refund of Caution deposit, get the application signed by the Warden and Managing Director and submit it in the office along with original receipt of the deposit paid.
      • Deposit not collected within six months of leaving the hostel be forfeited.
    6. No student shall be admitted to the hostel unless the required documents and fees are paid. If these are not paid by the appointed date, the admission is liable to be cancelled.
    7. MANDATORY CRITERIA: The hostelites who have been given the hostel accommodation must maintain high standards of studying and behavior. Those whose performance in academics and behavior in the hostel and college do not meet the required standard, are liable to be refused admission to the hostel in the following term
    8. KINDLY NOTE: The Hostel facility/Room is allotted for a period of ONE YEAR only. In order to continue to stay in the hostel, the student needs to re-apply for the hostel facility only after he/she fulfils the Mandatory criteria of the hostel i.e. 95% Attendance, good Academics record and Discipline. Any student who is an attendance defaulter/academic defaulter will be asked to leave the hostel.
  2. FEES
    1. The hostel term fees is announced at the beginning of the term. The Management holds the right to increase or decrease the hostel fees considering all the expenses involved.
    2. Fees should be paid in full at the beginning of the year.
    3. Failing to do so, the Management holds the right to discontinue hostel facility for the concerned student, after reviewing the case.
    4. Refund of Hostel Fees:
      • Hostel fees is payable, for the full year, at the start of the year. If any student quits the hostel accommodation, for their personal reasons, then the hostel fees already paidby them for that period will not be refundable.
  3. END OF ACADEMIC YEAR
    1. At the end of the academic year, all students must vacate their rooms and take away their belongings.
    2. Those who fulfil the Mandatory criteria (page 01, point number 07) and wish to continue in the Hostel for the next academic year, have to complete the re-admission procedures.
  4. RE- ADMISSION
    1. Re-admission to hostel and room shuffling shall be done at the end of every year
    2. Steps of Re-admission:
      • Students seeking re-admission in the hostel must fill the Re-Admission Form
      • Get class teachers and HODâ€™s signature on the form for academic and attendance record.
      • Pay the hostel fees for the next academic year.
      • Submit the duly filled and signed undertaking to the Warden.
    3. Re-admission is based on good behavior, academic performance, and 95% attendance.
      • Good behavior (observed by the authorities during the previous year- in the Hostel, canteen and in the campus).
      • Good academics performance.
      • 95% attendance.
    4. Students with ATKT or drop out will not be re-admitted in the hostel
    5. The Management and the warden will take the final decision to allot and decide rooms for the students, keeping in mind the safety and discipline regulations. Only when the clearance is given by the authorities, a student can occupy the given room in the hostel.
    6. Mutual transfer of rooms between hostelites or shifting of furniture, is not allowed under any circumstances.
  5. ACADEMIC REQUIREMENTS AND CODE OF CONDUCT:
    1. Hostelites must be on time for the college assembly at 8:30am.
    2. 95% attendance is compulsory for all hostelites in college.
    3. Hostelites must use the library for study purposes only.
    4. All hostelites must pass and clear all semester exams with good scores.
    5. Submissions must be made on time.
    6. Good behavior in college is a must, and grievances should be reported to the warden.
  6. GOING OUT OF THE CAMPUS:
    1. General Rules:
      • Hostelites are permitted to leave the campus after college hours and during holidays.
      • It is mandatory to enter their name, time of departure, and time of return in the Security Register.
      • No one is allowed to make entries or sign for another person.
      • All hostelites must return to the campus before 7:30pm.
    2. Extraordinary Permissions:
      • For late returns after 7:30pm, written approval from the Warden and Managing Director is required.
      • For outings, a written application and WhatsApp confirmation from parents is needed.
    3. Hostelites going home must fill the leave form and get approval from parents.
  7. MESS RULES:
    1. Joining the mess is compulsory for all hostelites.
    2. No sleeveless vests are allowed in the canteen.
    3. No food should be taken into rooms without permission from the warden.
    4. Food timings must be strictly followed. Late arrivals will not be served.
    5. No one is allowed meals beyond 8:30pm without permission.
    6. Grievances about the food or canteen staff should be reported to the warden.
  8. VISITORS TO THE HOSTEL
    1. Day-scholars and outsiders are not allowed into the hostel without prior notice.
    2. No guest is allowed overnight stay in the hostel.
    3. The authorities and hostel staff are authorized to enter the rooms when necessary.
    4. Occupants of one boyâ€™s hostel block are not allowed to visit another block without permission.
  9. ROOM, CLEANLINESS & HYGIENE
    1. Switch off the fans and lights when leaving the room.
    2. No cooking is allowed in the rooms.
    3. No electrical appliances are allowed except table lamps.
    4. No nails, pictures, or ropes may be affixed to the walls.
    5. Clothes must be dried in the designated area.
    6. Rooms must be kept neat and tidy.
    7. Beds must be made neatly, and garbage must be disposed of in dustbins outside the rooms.
  10. RAGGING, SMOKING, DRINKING, DRUGS
    1. Ragging in any form is strictly prohibited.
    2. Smoking and chewing of tobacco are strictly prohibited.
    3. Consumption or possession of alcohol or drugs is strictly prohibited.
    4. Students found in possession of any prohibited substances will be expelled.
